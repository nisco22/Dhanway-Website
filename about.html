About Dhanway Holdings:

We are a Company registered under the Companies Act of Zimbabwe [Chapter 24:03] and domiciled in Zimbabwe. We offer renewable energy solutions, with particular focus on solar supplies, installations and mantainance. Dhanway Holdings was founded in 2019, with the objective of providing affordable and sustainable renewable energy solutions amidst country-wide power challenges coupled with the ongoing war against climate change and environmental sustainability. we are commited to providing our clients with products of the highest quality at the most affordable and competetive prices, and an excellent return on investment.

Our mission is to lead the quest to change urban and rural areas in Zimbabwe as the trusted sustainability partner creating valued, single-sourced, effective and efficient energy solutions delivered with passion, expertise and a relentless focus on quality and consumer satisfaction.

Our Values are to innovatively seek continuous investment and growth in sustainable renewable energy while displaying the highest level of integrity, welcoming open and honest communication and challenging existing limitations.
